Home page

Hello there! Welcome to my site!
If you are here, that can only mean one thing: You want to learn everything you can about my wonderful little furball named Rocky!

As of at least September 19th 2023, Rocky is 12 years old. He is a plump little Russian Blue cat, and with that comes his beautiful grey fur and green eyes. Though his resting face is rather grumpy looking, he has a kind soul and kinder mannerisms.
My friends (correctly) find him to be very cute, and have even drawn artwork of him and I! He is a very anxious cat, and spends the vast majority of his time in my room snuggled up against me.

History

Rocket Jetstream Squirrel Palmer entered this world as all cats do.
Shortly after birth, he was given a name by his owners.
The name was "Mater".
This was because his purr was like a motor.
Around two months later, Rocky was brought to a new home as a birthday gift. The old owners were old friends with the new, and had given them two cats prior.
This time however, was special: It was a young cat-loving boy's 14th birthday, and my dear reader, that young boy... Was me.
Upon receiving this glorious gift, I quickly changed his name.
I wasn't all that fond of the name "Mater" as it comes from the Pixar movie "Cars".
I gave him a much better name: "Gorky".
Yes, you read that right. "Gorky".
Not the best of names I've picked, but in my defense, it came from one of the stuffed animals I loved playing with growing up.
Less than a week later, "Gorky" received yet another new name: "Rocky".
This one came from a show I and my siblings had been watching quite a bit of: "The Adventures of Rocky and Bullwinkle and Friends".
As Rocky grew, his adventurous side began to shine.
One day he was out exploring, and had a lovely time eating a small rodent.
A short time later, he began vomiting blood: the rodent had eaten an anti-blood-clotting poison, and now Rocky had eaten it along with its small pointy bones.
After a thorough investigation by the vet, I was happy to hear that he would be alright.
Despite this, I decided that from that day forward he would not be going outside unattended.
After purchasing a cat-sized leash and harness, Rocky was again permitted outside.
He became anxious as he grew older, and spent more and more time sleeping in my room.
He is most likely in my room right now!

Help

I can not express enough just how much Rocky has helped me throughout my life. He calmed me when I was anxious, he comforted me when I was distressed, and he yawned at me when it was getting late.
There have been times in my life when I was in very dark places, and had been seriously considering actions that would have ended poorly for me.
In those times, it was the thought of Rocky missing me that saved me.
I know with certainty that Rocky is a guardian angel to me, and I thank God for allowing me to care for my boy for over 12 years now, as they both continue to care for me.
