~~All About Me~~

Corry McConnell Palmer

AKA "That one guy everyone saw at the Temple a lot"

Hello there!
This is an about me page.

I was born and raised in southern California, and still live there to this day.

I sacrificed two years of my earthly state on a mission for God through the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.
I served in the San Diego Church Service Missionary Group. A few things I was tasked with include:
- General meetinghouse maintenance, such as cleaning up scratches in the woodwork, re-securing the pews to the ground, and changing lightbulbs,
- Serving in the local Family History Center and assisting the people there,
- and serving in the San Diego, California Temple in the Cafeteria and as an ordinance worker.

After my Missionary service concluded, I continued to serve in the Temple for another year, and was then hired to work in the kitchen. About a month later, Covid-19 caused everything to close.
Despite being unable to do food work, the Church continued to pay me throughout the time leading up to the official, permanent closure of all Temple cafeterias.
I also served in the laundry for part of that time.

I continued to serve as a Temple worker all the way to the extended closure for renovations, and am anxiously awaiting the day when I will return to worship and serve with God's children in His Holy house.
