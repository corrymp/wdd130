All About Rocky

Hello there! Welcome to my site! If you are here, that can only mean one thing: You want to learn everything you can about my wonderful little furball named Rocky! Where to beginâ€¦

As of at least September 29th 2023, Rocky is 12 years old. He is a plump little Russian Blue cat, and with that comes his beautiful grey fur and green eyes. Though his resting face is rather grumpy looking, he has a kind soul and kinder mannerisms.

My friends (correctly) find him to be very cute, and have even drawn artwork of him and I! He is a very anxious cat, and spends the vast majority of his time in my room snuggled up against me.

it is my hope that as you study the contents of this site, you will be able to understand even the smallest fragment of the love I have for the beautiful little flooffer named Rocky, and the sheer impact he has had on my life.

He is truly a blessing from He who made me.
