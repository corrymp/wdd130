History:

The Life of Rocky Thus Far

Rocket Jetstream Squirrel Palmer entered this world as all cats do. Shortly after birth, he was given a name by his owners. The name was "Mater". This was because his purr was like a motor.

Around two months later, Rocky was brought to a new home as a birthday gift. The old owners were old friends with the new, and had given them two cats prior. This time however, was special: It was a young cat-loving boy's 14th birthday, and my dear reader, that young boy... Was me.

Upon receiving this glorious gift, I quickly changed his name. I wasn't all that fond of the name "Mater" as it comes from the Pixar movie "Cars". I gave him a much better name: "Gorky".

Yes, you read that right. "Gorky". Not the best of names I've picked, but in my defense, it came from one of the stuffed animals I loved playing with growing up.

Less than a week later, "Gorky" received yet another new name: "Rocky". This one came from a show I and my siblings had been watching quite a bit of: "The Adventures of Rocky and Bullwinkle and Friends".

A Bit of Danger

As Rocky grew, his adventurous side began to shine.
One day he was out exploring, and had a lovely time eating a small rodent.
A short time later, he began vomiting blood: the rodent had eaten an anti-blood-clotting poison, and now Rocky had eaten it along with its small pointy bones.

After a thorough investigation by the vet, I was happy to hear that he would be alright.
Despite this, I decided that from that day forward he would not be going outside unattended.
After purchasing a cat-sized leash and harness, Rocky was again permitted outside.

He became anxious as he grew older, and spent more and more time sleeping in my room.
He is most likely in my room right now!

Rocky is still alive, and his story continues.
